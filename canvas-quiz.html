Built for School Privacy

Your Canvas credentials never touch our database.

Stateless server. Browser-only credentials.

Most Canvas tools store your API token on their server. We don't. Your Canvas URL and personal access token live in your browser's localStorage only — the same place your browser stores any other website's preferences. When you click "Push to Canvas," the token flows through our app for that single API call, then is discarded.

We don't run a database. We don't store student data (the app never sees student answers — Canvas handles those). We don't store your quizzes. The only thing our server ever sees about you is your email (for subscription verification) and the quiz content during the brief moment it's being assembled for upload.

You can clear all stored values anytime by clicking "Reset Settings." You can revoke the API token in your Canvas account at any moment.
