Questions

FAQs

What if my school doesn't use Canvas?

Right now Euclidia Quiz is built specifically for Canvas LMS. If you use Schoology, Google Classroom, Blackboard, or another platform, you can still use the tool. Just download the quiz as QTI .zip or as a printable Word doc instead of using the one-click Canvas push. We may add direct integration for other LMSes later based on demand.

Do I need a Gmail account to sign in?

No. You need a Google account, but Google accounts can be created with any email address, including your school email. Most US K-12 schools on Google Workspace for Education already provide one automatically. If your school is on Microsoft 365 and you don't have a personal Google account, creating one at accounts.google.com takes 60 seconds.

Is the math really correct? AI tools have burned me before.

Yes. That is the whole point. Every problem and its answer is professionally authored and verified before they reach your students, not invented on the fly. The same content foundation powers our lesson catalog on TPT (used by hundreds of teachers). It produces clean integer or fractional answers and uses wrong-answer choices that reflect the actual mistakes students tend to make on each problem type. If you ever find a question where the math is wrong, email us and we'll fix it within 24 hours.

Do you store student data?

No. Student data (names, grades, submissions, anything personally identifiable) never reaches our servers. Students take the quiz inside your Canvas course; Canvas auto-grades it; we never see any of that. The only data we hold is your email address (for subscription verification, kept in Stripe) and your subscription status. Your Canvas API token lives in your browser's localStorage only.

What does the free trial actually include?

Full access, same as paid. Build unlimited quizzes, push to Canvas, download QTI or Word docs. Stripe takes your card up front (their subscription model requires it), but the first charge happens on day 3 unless you cancel. Euclidia never sees or stores your card. Cancel anytime in your billing portal. One click, no email required.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Click "Manage subscription" in the app sidebar. That takes you to Stripe's billing portal where you can cancel in one click. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No emails, no haggling, no retention dark patterns.

Why $6.99? That seems specific.

It's roughly the cost of two coffees a month, which we figured was about right for a tool that saves you 5-10 hours of quiz building per month. The annual plan ($59.99/yr) works out to about $5/mo if you want to lock in. We're a bootstrapped one-person operation; pricing covers hosting, infrastructure, and occasional sleep.

Built for School Privacy

Your Canvas credentials never touch our database.

Stateless server. Browser-only credentials.

Most Canvas tools store your API token on their server. We don't. Your Canvas URL and personal access token live in your browser's localStorage only, the same place your browser stores any other website's preferences. When you click "Push to Canvas," the token flows through our app for that single API call, then is discarded.

We don't run a database. We don't store student data (the app never sees student answers; Canvas handles those). We don't store your quizzes. The only thing our server ever sees about you is your email (for subscription verification) and the quiz content during the brief moment it's being assembled for upload.

You can clear all stored values anytime by clicking "Reset Settings." You can revoke the API token in your Canvas account at any moment.
