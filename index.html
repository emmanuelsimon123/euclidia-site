The Real Math

What if your time was worth twice as much?

Here's something nobody says out loud.

Your raise this year was probably somewhere between 1 and 3 percent. Inflation was right there with it, often ahead of it. That's a pay cut disguised as a pay raise.

Euclidia can't change your hourly rate. That's not in our hands.

But what if you could cut your weekly prep from 50 hours to 25? The effective value of every hour you put in just doubled. Same salary. Half the time. That's not marketing. That's arithmetic.

We built Euclidia on the belief that teachers deserve more. Our hope is that our resources are an expression of our labor of love for teachers and students. We want to serve you, so you can serve your students.

Our Mission

Built by one teacher who is right there in the trenches with you.

Euclidia exists because teachers do the most important work for underappreciated pay. I built this as an individual teacher, not a corporate publisher, after too many nights spent doing prep work that could have been done faster and better. Our mission is simple. We want to give you your time back so you can teach with energy, be present for your people, and end the day feeling human.
