Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: Euclidia Outreach is a web app. Student data is processed in memory only for the duration of your session and is never written to a database or saved to disk. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information or your students' data.

1. Who We Are

Euclidia is an independent teacher resource company operated by an individual educator. We offer math lesson plan resources and a custom lesson generator through our website, and teacher automation software under the Euclidia Outreach product line. You can reach us at euclidiamath@gmail.com.

2. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy covers all Euclidia products and services:

  • Euclidia website: euclidiamath.com, including the shop and lesson plan resources
  • Euclidia Generator: our web-based custom lesson bundle generator
  • Euclidia Outreach: our web-based teacher outreach tool

3. Euclidia Website and Shop

When you visit euclidiamath.com:

  • We do not run advertising or sell your data to third parties.
  • Standard server logs may record your IP address and browser type for security purposes. These are not shared or sold.
  • Shop purchases are processed through Payhip and Teachers Pay Teachers. We do not store your payment information. Please review Payhip's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.
  • If you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry.

4. Euclidia Generator

The Euclidia Generator is a web-based tool that generates custom lesson plan bundles from Common Core standards you enter. Here is how we handle data in the Generator:

  • The Generator processes the standards, grade level, and settings you enter in order to generate lesson content. This input is sent to an AI generation service to produce your lesson bundle.
  • No student data is ever entered into or processed by the Generator. It is a curriculum creation tool, not a student data tool.
  • Generator credit purchases are processed through Payhip. We do not store your payment information.
  • We may retain account information such as your email address and remaining credit balance in order to provide the service.

5. Euclidia Outreach

Euclidia Outreach is a web app you use in your browser. No download, no installation required. You sign in with your Google account, and the app runs on a secure server that processes your data only while you are actively using it.

Student data is never stored. When you upload a gradebook or roster CSV, that data is held in the app's memory only for the duration of your session. It is never written to a database, never saved to disk, and never shared with any third party by Euclidia. The moment you close the tab or sign out, it is permanently gone. No copy is kept anywhere.

Gmail API. Euclidia Outreach uses Google's Gmail API to create draft emails in your own Gmail or Google Workspace account. When drafts are created, the email content passes through Google's servers as part of normal Gmail operation. If your school email runs on Google Workspace, Google is already a FERPA-compliant service provider for your district. If you use a personal Gmail account, please review Google's Privacy Policy. Euclidia does not receive or store any of this data.

Google sign-in. When you sign in, you authorize Euclidia Outreach to create Gmail drafts on your behalf. This authorization is limited to draft creation only. The app cannot read your email, access your files, or perform any other action in your account. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.

Account information. We store only your Google account email address to identify your session. No student names, grades, or emails are ever stored by Euclidia. We retain account and contact information only as long as needed to provide the service or respond to your inquiry, and we will delete it on request.

Limited Use. Euclidia Outreach's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Google user data for advertising, do not sell it, and do not allow humans to read it except as required for security, to comply with the law, or where you have given explicit consent for specific items.

6. FERPA Compliance

Euclidia Outreach is designed with FERPA in mind. Student education records you upload are processed in memory only for the duration of your session. They are never written to a database, saved to disk, or retained by Euclidia after your session ends. You, as the educator, remain in full control of your students' data at all times.

The teacher acts as the data controller for any student information they choose to upload. Euclidia does not collect student data directly from students, does not determine how it is used, and does not retain it. We act solely as a tool you operate under your own professional and district obligations.

7. For School Administrators and District IT

If you are reviewing Euclidia Outreach on behalf of a school or district, here is the short version. A teacher uploads a gradebook or roster. The tool processes it in memory only, for the length of that session, to generate draft emails in the teacher's own Google account. Nothing is written to a database or disk, nothing is retained after the session ends, and no student data is sold, shared, or used for advertising. The teacher remains in control of the data at all times.

We are happy to support district review. A data processing agreement is available for districts on request. Email us at euclidiamath@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

8. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Euclidia Outreach does not use cookies.

9. Third-Party Links

Our site and software may contain links to third-party services such as Google, Payhip, and Teachers Pay Teachers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies directly.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections using industry-standard HTTPS and TLS. For Euclidia Outreach, the strongest security measure is the design itself: student data is never written to a database or disk, and is discarded the moment your session ends. Because nothing is stored at rest, there is no retained copy to breach.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at euclidiamath.com/privacy.html. Continued use of our products after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact
