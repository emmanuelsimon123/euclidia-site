Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: Euclidia Outreach runs entirely on your computer. Your student data never leaves your machine. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information or your students' data.

1. Who We Are

Euclidia is an independent teacher resource company operated by an individual educator. We offer math lesson plan resources and a custom lesson generator through our website, and teacher automation software under the Euclidia Outreach product line. You can reach us at euclidiamath@gmail.com.

2. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy covers all Euclidia products and services:

  • Euclidia website — euclidiamath.com, including the shop and lesson plan resources
  • Euclidia Generator — our web-based custom lesson bundle generator
  • Euclidia Outreach — our downloadable local teacher automation software

3. Euclidia Website and Shop

When you visit euclidiamath.com:

  • We do not run advertising or sell your data to third parties.
  • Standard server logs may record your IP address and browser type for security purposes. These are not shared or sold.
  • Shop purchases are processed through Payhip and Teachers Pay Teachers. We do not store your payment information. Please review Payhip's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.
  • If you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry.

4. Euclidia Generator

The Euclidia Generator is a web-based tool that generates custom lesson plan bundles from Common Core standards you enter. Here is how we handle data in the Generator:

  • The Generator processes the standards, grade level, and settings you enter in order to generate lesson content. This input is sent to an AI generation service to produce your lesson bundle.
  • No student data is ever entered into or processed by the Generator. It is a curriculum creation tool, not a student data tool.
  • Generator credit purchases are processed through Payhip. We do not store your payment information.
  • We may retain account information such as your email address and remaining credit balance in order to provide the service.

5. Euclidia Outreach — Local Software

Euclidia Outreach is software that runs entirely on your local Windows computer. It does not transmit your data to Euclidia or any third party operated by us.

Student data stays on your machine. When you upload a gradebook or roster CSV, that data is processed locally on your computer. It is never sent to our servers, stored in a database, or shared with any third party by Euclidia.

Gmail API (if used). The Gmail version of Euclidia Outreach uses Google's Gmail API to create draft emails in your own Gmail or Google Workspace account. When drafts are created, the email content passes through Google's servers as part of normal Gmail operation. If your school email runs on Google Workspace, Google is already a FERPA-compliant service provider for your district. If you use a personal Gmail account, please review Google's Privacy Policy. Euclidia does not receive or store any of this data.

Microsoft Outlook (if used). The Outlook version creates drafts using Classic Outlook automation running entirely on your local machine. No data leaves your computer through this method.

Credentials. The Google OAuth credentials file included with Euclidia Outreach is limited in scope to creating Gmail drafts only. It cannot read your email, access your files, or perform any other action.

6. FERPA Compliance

Euclidia Outreach is designed with FERPA in mind. Because the software runs locally on your machine, student education records processed by the app are not transmitted to or stored by Euclidia. You, as the educator, remain in full control of your students' data at all times. We strongly recommend following your school or district's acceptable use policies when using any software that handles student information.

7. Children's Privacy

Euclidia products are designed for use by educators, not directly by students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The student data processed by Euclidia Outreach is handled locally on the teacher's machine and is never transmitted to us.

8. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Euclidia Outreach does not use cookies.

9. Third-Party Links

Our site and software may contain links to third-party services such as Google, Payhip, and Teachers Pay Teachers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies directly.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle. For Euclidia Outreach, the best security measure is the design itself. Student data never leaves your machine in the first place.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at euclidiamath.com/privacy.html. Continued use of our products after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at euclidiamath@gmail.com.
