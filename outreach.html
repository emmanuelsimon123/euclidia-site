How It Works

Three quick steps.

1

Build your roster

Export your class roster from Canvas, PowerSchool, or any LMS that exports a CSV. Run the Roster Builder once at the start of term, or any time your roster changes.

One time setup
2

Upload your gradebook

Export your gradebook as a CSV from your LMS. Open Outreach, load the file, and the tool identifies every student who needs attention based on the thresholds you set.

Under 2 minutes
3

Send from your inbox

Choose Outlook or Gmail. Outreach generates personalized messages for every flagged student and parent, then creates drafts directly in your inbox. Review, adjust if you want, and send.

Under 5 minutes total
