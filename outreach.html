Built for School Privacy

Your student data is never stored. Anywhere.

When you upload a gradebook, that data is held in memory only for the duration of your session. It is never written to a database or saved to disk. The moment you close the tab or sign out, it's gone and no copy is kept anywhere. No tracking. What happens in your gradebook stays in your gradebook. This matters because student data is sensitive, and we take that seriously.

Most education tools collect and store far more student data than they need to. We did the opposite. Euclidia Outreach holds none of it, so there is no database of student records to leak, sell, or breach. FERPA exists to keep schools in control of student data. Our design keeps that control with you, the teacher, by never holding the data in the first place.
